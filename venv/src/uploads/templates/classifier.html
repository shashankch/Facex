Face Recognition Process(using Classifier):

  • Step 1: Face detection using HOG(Histogram of oriented gradients).
  • Step 2: Posing and Projecting Faces using Face Landmark Estimation Technique(68 specific points).
  • Step 3: Face Encoding (128 measurements of face/Embedding) using Pretrained Deep Neural Network(DLib-Resnet model).
  • Step 4: Finding Person's name from the Face Encoding using SVM Classifier.
